# Sidebar - User Documentation

## Overview

The sidebar provides easy navigation throughout the application and quick access to your account settings.

## Navigation

The sidebar contains the following menu items:

| Item | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Dashboard | Your main overview page |
| Documents | Document management (planned) |
| Team | Team collaboration (planned) |
| Settings | Account and app preferences |

## Using the Sidebar

### Desktop

- The sidebar is always visible on the left side
- Click any menu item to navigate to that page
- Active page is highlighted

### Mobile

- Click the menu icon (hamburger) in the header to open the sidebar
- Sidebar slides in from the left
- Click outside or navigate to close

## User Menu

Click on your profile at the bottom of the sidebar to access:

### Profile Options
- **Profile** - View your profile (planned)
- **Settings** - Go to settings page

### Theme Selection
- **Light** - Always use light theme
- **Dark** - Always use dark theme
- **System** - Match your device settings

### Sign Out
- Click **Sign out** to log out of your account
- You'll be redirected to the login page

## Your Profile Display

The sidebar footer shows:
- Your initials in a colored circle
- Your full name
- Your email address

## Collapsing the Sidebar

On desktop, you can collapse the sidebar to just icons by clicking the toggle button in the header. This gives you more space for content.
