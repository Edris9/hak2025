# Theme - User Documentation

## Overview

You can customize the application's appearance by choosing between light, dark, or system themes.

## Theme Options

| Theme | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Light | Bright background with dark text |
| Dark | Dark background with light text |
| System | Automatically matches your device settings |

## How to Change Theme

### From User Menu

1. Click on your profile at the bottom of the sidebar
2. Hover over "Theme" in the dropdown menu
3. Select your preferred theme (Light, Dark, or System)

### From Settings Page

1. Navigate to **Settings** from the sidebar
2. Find the **Appearance** section
3. Click the button for your preferred theme

## Persistence

Your theme preference is saved automatically and will be remembered:
- When you refresh the page
- When you close and reopen the browser
- Across different sessions

## System Theme

When "System" is selected:
- The app follows your operating system's theme
- If your device switches to dark mode at night, the app will too
- Changes automatically when your system theme changes

## Troubleshooting

### Theme not changing?
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser's local storage
- Check if your browser blocks local storage

### Theme flashing on load?
This is normal for a brief moment during page load. The app quickly applies your saved preference.
